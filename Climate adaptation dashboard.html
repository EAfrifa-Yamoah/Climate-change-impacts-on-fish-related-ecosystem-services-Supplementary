Network Analysis: Climate Adaptation Strategies

Interconnected approaches for fish-related ecosystem services

E. Afrifa-Yamoah et al.

Network Overview

This network shows the interconnections between climate adaptation strategies. Node size indicates implementation readiness, while edge thickness shows relationship strength. Click and drag nodes to explore connections.

Key Network Insights:
• Early Warning Systems serve as information hubs
• EBFM acts as central coordination mechanism
• Technology enables multiple other strategies
• Governance remains the most challenging to implement
